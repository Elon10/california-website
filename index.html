F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • Can we partner?

    We normally will pick specific servers deemed worthy of a partnership with us.But, if you would like you could purchase a paid ad in 「🛒」store.

  • How do I become a moderator?

    You can become a moderator once staff applications are open and released to the public, normally you will be able to find them in 「📖」information.

  • Do I have to apply for a departments such as Fire/Police/Sheriff/EMS/etc?

    No, you are not required to unless you want to be apart of a sub-division such as SWAT, FBI, etc.

  • Is this server VC only?

    Not at the moment. The server is not a VC only server at this time, but is subject to change in the future. If you are interested in a VC only roleplay join our whitelisted server!

  • Where do I report a staff member?

    To report a staff member you must head over to our support server and information channel, click appeal hub then click on staff report form.

  • When will my application be read?

    Applications will be read on Fridays - Sundays when staff have the availability to read applications. Asking for applications to be read will result in a immediate denial of your application.

  • How do I join the server?

    To join the server head over to ER:LC. Once you have loaded in go to Menu > Join by code > Enter: CasRP > Then your done!

  • When will the server go up?

    The server will go up around 12pm. We will normally host 2-3 times a day.
